Evaluation

The evaluation portion of this WebQuest examines seven categories ranging from the content of the final product to the productivity of the provided class time. Each category in the rubric is equally important in our 21st Century and thus scored equally. The rubric also includes a comments section for each evaluated category but it is strongly recommended that one-on-one conferences or group conferences be provided by the teacher. Please note: the rubric will print on two pages unless you print on 9x17 paper.

The original rubric can be easily modified to meet various purposes and learning outcomes.

For a quick reference, click on the evaluation link to view the online rubric that the students see (evaluation.htm).
