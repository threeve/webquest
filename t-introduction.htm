Introduction

This WebQuest was developed as part of a graduate course assignment for Olivet Nazarene University.

With this WebQuest, students will be able to learn more about the different types of figurative language by collaborating with their peers and by constructing a lesson that they can use to teach the other students in the classroom. The repetition of types of figurative language examples and the group presentations will allow students to become actively engaged in learning about the different types of figurative language.
