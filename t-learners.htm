Learners

This WebQuest is designed for a typical seventh grade language arts class consisting of 28 students ranging in academic performance. Students who have a paraprofessional written in their IEP will be provided support to help foster the learning process, but the supporting individual will not be considered an additional member of the learning team. This lesson is anchored in seventh grade language arts and requires the students to actively participate in the learning of different types of figurative language.

Prior to beginning this lesson the students should already have prior knowledge in PowerPoint and be proficient in slide elements. Students should also understand how to alter a template in order to present new information.

The students will need to have prior instruction in citing sources for their "Works Cited" slide in their PowerPoint.
