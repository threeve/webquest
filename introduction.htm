Introduction

In this WebQuest you will explore 9 different types of figurative language:

  • Metaphor
  • Simile
  • Onomatopoeia
  • Personification
  • Alliteration
  • Hyperbole
  • Idiom
  • Oxymoron
  • Assonance
  • Cliche - Do not choose this one; an example PowerPoint is available on the Process page that already teaches this type.

After completing the activities in this WebQuest you will be able to create a PowerPoint demonstrating your knowledge of several of the types of figurative language listed above.
